Useful AI for the Automobile Industry
Data Mining 3.0

Fully automated data mining, customer contact and response handling. Critically, BlinkAI closes the loop by reaching the customer AND handling the customers response- actually scheduling sales and service appointments.

Useful AI does a job

While Chatbots are good at providing answers to known questions, they fall short of performing useful work. A chatbot can answer the question “What are the service department hours?” With useful AI, Blink’s answer would be “7a to 6p, would you like for me to schedule an appointment?”

It’s about Time- identify key moments in the ownership lifecycle

Blink identifies important moments in the ownership lifecycle- Service is Due, Lease is Ending, Factory Warranty is Expiring and dozens more of those moments. Blink reaches customers at the right time.

The Right Place? It’s about Mobile.

Gain the customer’s attention by sending relevant messages via Text, Facebook Messenger and Voicemail to their Smartphone. Unlike email or snail mail, these messages get read. Mobile is not specific to Gen Z, Millennials, Gen X or Baby Boomers, it has managed to infiltrate all generations. And while some uses may differ, consumers from all generations can be reached on mobile

  • 82% of people say they “read every text message” (Twilio)
  • 9 out of 10 customers say they would like to use messaging to talk to business (Twilio)
  • Response rates from SMS are 209% higher than from phone calls, Facebook, or email (Emarsys)
  • Facebook Messenger is the #1 app in the US and Canada (facebook)
  • Facebook Messenger has an 80% open rate and 4x-10x the click-thru rate of email.
  • Voicemail Drops have a 96% listen rate (foundlocal)
  • Compare to Email open rates of 23% and a click-thru rate of 2.7% (Mailchimp)

Leverage AI to provide a highly personalized and convenient experience

Because we access multiple databases, we know the customer’s name, vehicle, purchase history, service history and more. “Hello Lauren, our records indicate it is time for the 15,000 mile service on your 2017 Chevrolet Equinox. I found a coupon for 10% off. Would you like to schedule now?” Because we know the customer, all we need is when. No need for the customer to enter more information- or even worse- remember a login and password just to schedule service.

Leverage AI to schedule appointments

via FB Messenger, Text or Telephone. Ad it to their calendar then automatically send an appointment confirmation. “Lauren’s appointment for 15,000 mile maintenance on 2017 Chevrolet Equinox is Thursday, April 6th at 8:30 AM. Reply “C” to confirm. Reply “R” to reschedule. Call 555-123-4567 with questions”. Chatbots cannot do that.

Leverage AI to cross-sell, up-sell and offer incentives

  • Buy a pre-paid maintenance contract and save 20% off your required maintenance
  • Your factory warranty is expiring. Get 10% off an extended warranty contract now
  • Your 25,000-mile maintenance service is due. I found a coupon for 15% off. Would you like to schedule now?

Open 24/7

Connect when the customer wants to connect using the channel the customer wants to use Text, FB Messenger, phone or website

Don’t miss a call- No wait times, no call transfers, no lost calls

Calls do not come in evenly. They tend to come all at the same time. Blink can eliminate hold and wait times for customers scheduling service and let your service advisor focus on the customers already in service.

Make it Automated

No campaigns to set up or manage, no teams to train, no administrator to “run” the software. Blink is completely automated.
